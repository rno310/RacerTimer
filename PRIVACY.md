# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 24, 2026**

This policy applies to the **Pin to Win** mobile application (the "App") for Apple Watch and iPhone, published by Arnaud Benahmed (the "Developer").

## Summary

Pin to Win does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs.

## What information the App handles

The App stores only the following on your device, and only on your device:

- Your selected starting countdown duration (3 minutes or 5 minutes).
- The current state of the timer (running, paused, count-up).

This information stays on the iPhone and Apple Watch on which the App is installed. It is never transmitted to the Developer or to any third party.

## Communication between your devices

When the App is installed on both an iPhone and a paired Apple Watch belonging to the same user, the two devices keep their timer state in sync using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This communication happens directly between your own devices over Bluetooth or Wi-Fi, mediated by Apple's operating system. The Developer does not operate any servers and receives no data from this communication.

## Data the App does not collect

To be explicit, the App does **not** collect, request, or transmit any of the following:

- Personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, address)
- Location data
- Contacts, calendar, photos, microphone, or camera data
- Health, fitness, or biometric data
- Device identifiers, advertising identifiers, or analytics events
- Crash reports
- Usage statistics

## Permissions

The App does not request any system permissions beyond what is required for it to play sounds and haptics on your device.

## Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children. Because the App collects no data of any kind, it inherently complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and equivalent laws regarding children's data.

## Third parties

The App contains no third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, or tracking technologies.

## Changes to this policy

If the Developer ever changes the App in a way that changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated and the change will be called out in the App Store release notes for that version.
